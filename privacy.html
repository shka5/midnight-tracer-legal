Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-07-04

Midnight Tracer ("the Service") is an anonymous community that does not require registration. This policy explains what information we process and how.

1. Information We Collect

• Device identifier (device_id): a randomly generated string used for anonymous posting, deletion, and blocking
• User-generated content: posts, comments, optional photos/videos, nicknames
• Location data: used to calculate distance and run location-based detection tools (such as the spirit radar). Not used to browse a map. Location-based calculations are processed on-device. Haunted-spot posts are written as location-undisclosed narrative text, and coordinates are not stored on the server
• Microphone (audio): some detection tools use the microphone for sound analysis, and that analysis is processed entirely on-device and not transmitted. However, if you use the manual screen-recording feature, microphone audio may be saved into the recorded video file, and if you choose to upload or share that video, the audio embedded in it may be transmitted along with it. The Ghost Box and Spirit Board voice-recognition features use your device OS's speech-recognition service (Apple/Google), and depending on your device and language settings, audio data may be sent to that OS provider's servers for processing
• Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., category view counts)

We do not collect direct contact identifiers such as name, email, or phone number. IP addresses are not stored directly at the application server level, but infrastructure providers (Supabase, Cloudflare, etc.) may process access logs for security and reliability according to their own policies.

2. How We Use Information

• To provide the Service (posting, displaying, deleting content)
• To prevent abuse (rate limiting, report handling, auto-hiding)
• Anonymous analytics (popular categories, active users)

We do not use collected information for any purpose beyond the above.

3. Retention

• device_id: until you clear the app data. However, a device identifier subject to a restriction (ban) is retained separately, independent of clearing app data, to prevent evading the restriction by re-registering, and is deleted once the restriction is lifted
• Posts/comments: hidden immediately when deleted by the author. May be retained on our servers for a limited period for abuse prevention and legal-dispute handling before removal

4. Processing Vendors and International Transfer

The Service may entrust processing to the following overseas infrastructure vendors, and data is transferred internationally on an ongoing basis over secure network connections whenever you use community, diagnostics, or anti-abuse features:

• Supabase, Inc. (United States, https://supabase.com/privacy): database, authentication, and anonymous account management. Data may include device_id, posts, comments, nicknames, reports, blocks, and moderation metadata. Retention: until the period set in §3 (Retention) of this policy, or until the processing purpose ends or you request deletion, whichever is sooner (backups at the vendor may cause a short additional delay before full erasure)
• Cloudflare, Inc. (United States and other regions, https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/): R2 media storage, Turnstile anti-bot checks, security, and delivery infrastructure. Data may include uploaded media, media keys, device/browser signals, and access logs. Retention: until the content is deleted or the account restriction is lifted (backups at the vendor may cause a short additional delay)
• Functional Software, Inc. d/b/a Sentry (United States, https://sentry.io/privacy/): crash/error diagnostics. Data may include device/OS information, error logs, and app state. We intentionally do not include direct contact identifiers such as name, email, or phone number. Retention: until the diagnostic purpose is served; detailed retention follows Sentry's own policy

You may object to international transfer, but some or all community upload, diagnostics, or anti-abuse features may become unavailable. Contact iconic.app.official@gmail.com for refusal or deletion requests; we will respond without undue delay, generally within 10 days absent exceptional circumstances. Community media is not currently auto-screened by external AI such as Google Cloud; this policy and store privacy disclosures will be updated before such moderation is enabled. Tool photo analysis runs entirely on-device.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. Tool photo analysis runs entirely on-device, so your photos are not sent to any external AI. We may comply with valid government or legal requests as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
• Delete your content (via in-app delete or password)
• Block other users (menu > block)
• Report content (menu > report)
• Request inquiries/responses: iconic.app.official@gmail.com

7. Minors

This Service is not intended for users under the age of 14. We do not provide a verifiable parental-consent flow for children under 14, so those users may not use the Service. In jurisdictions with higher consent ages, we follow local law.

8. Notification of Changes

Material changes to this policy will be announced in-app 7 days in advance. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact: iconic.app.official@gmail.com
