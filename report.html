Report Content

Effective: 2026-07-04

Midnight Tracer is an anonymous community. If you find a post, comment, photo, or video that violates our Community Guidelines or the law, you can report it using the methods below. You can report even without installing the app or signing in.

Report in the app

If you are using the app, the "Report" button on each post or comment is the fastest way. Content is auto-hidden for admin review once enough reports accumulate, or immediately for severe reports.

Report without the app (email)

Email iconic.app.official@gmail.com with:
• Where the content is (a link to the post/comment, or a screenshot)
• The reason (e.g., violence, hate, sexual content, copyright, privacy exposure, defamation)
• (Optional) how we can reach you for a reply
We review and act within a reasonable time and reply where appropriate.

Illegal or urgent reports

For content that is clearly illegal or urgent — child sexual abuse material (CSAM), sexual abuse, or imminent threats of harm — email iconic.app.official@gmail.com with [URGENT] in the subject. Such reports are prioritized and hidden immediately, and may be reported to law enforcement or relevant authorities with evidence preserved as required by law.

Report / contact: iconic.app.official@gmail.com
