Community Guidelines

Effective: 2026-07-04

We are anonymous but build this space together. Please follow these principles.

Encouraged Behavior

• Share diverse experiences with curiosity
• Respect others' experiences
• Distinguish fact from speculation
• Take responsibility for your posts

Prohibited Content

• Graphic violence/gore
• Self-harm/suicide promotion
• Sexual content involving minors
• Hate/discrimination (race/gender/religion/disability)
• Personal information disclosure (real names, addresses, contacts, photos)
• Fraud or solicitation
• Spam or advertising

Reporting

If you find inappropriate content, tap the ⋮ menu on the post/comment and select "Report". When 10 or more reports accumulate, content is auto-hidden. Moderators review within a reasonable period where operationally possible.

Report reasons: Spam/Advertising · Inappropriate content · Hate speech · Misinformation · Defamation · Personal info exposure · Adult content · Copyright infringement · Other

Blocking

If a user repeatedly posts uncomfortable content, select "Block this user" from the menu. Their posts and comments will no longer be visible to you.

Enforcement

• Stage 1: Auto-hide (10 accumulated reports)
• Stage 2: Permanent removal after moderator review
• Stage 3: Content removal and author block for repeated violations

Appeals

If your content was removed/hidden unfairly, contact iconic.app.official@gmail.com with the post ID. We respond within a reasonable period where operationally possible.

Contact: iconic.app.official@gmail.com
